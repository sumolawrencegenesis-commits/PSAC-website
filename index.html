Our Core Mission

"The Progressive Student Advocacy Club is a student-led organization that equips students to excel academically, develop strong leadership skills, and cultivate moral integrity. Our mission is to provide a supportive platform for students to grow through developmental initiatives on campus, learn essential life skills, and develop into responsible individuals who excel in and out of the classroom, while creating opportunities to build a lasting legacy."

Our goal is to help students succeed in school and in life, and make a positive impact in their communities.

How We Help Students Grow:

Academic Excellence

Do well in school through dedicated support and resources.

Leadership Development

Become good leaders by developing essential life and soft skills.

Moral Integrity

Be honest and responsible, cultivating strong moral character.

Our Purpose and Impact

Club Objectives

  • • Help Students resolve minor and major issues in various subjects when necessary.
  • • Handle initiatives for development on the school campus.
  • • Teach students the value of education.
  • • Prepare students for the future through relevant skills.

Club Activities

  • • Organizing workshops focused on skill development and academic success.
  • • Providing opportunities to interact with successful individuals and gain motivation from their success stories.
  • • Leading campus improvement and community initiatives.

Membership Details

The club is open to all students who share the club's mission and objectives. Members are expected to participate actively in club activities and contribute consistently to the club's goals.

How to Get Your Public Link

To make this website available to everyone, you need to host it online. Once hosted, that public link is what you can share.

Step 1: Save the current `index.html` file on your computer.

Step 2: Upload this single file to a free hosting service like **GitHub Pages** (e.g., in a repository named `PSAC-website`).

Step 3: Once deployed, the service will provide you with a public URL, which will look something like this (This is just an example, your actual link will be different):

https://yourusername.github.io/PSAC-website/

**This final link is the one you share with others!**
